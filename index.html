Log Electricity Consumption per region — kWh usage and billing amount in Php. Auto-calculates GHG emissions (tCO2) and equivalent trees needed to offset.
Log Water Consumption per region — Domestic Use (m³), Potable Use (Liters), and Industrial Use (m³), each with corresponding amount in Php.
